The Eiffel Tower is an iconic wrought-iron lattice tower in Paris, France, renowned worldwide as a symbol of French culture and architectural ingenuity.Designed by the engineer Gustave Eiffel, it stands approximately 324 meters (1,063 feet) tall, making it one of the tallest structures in Paris.

Bermuda Triangle, section of the North Atlantic Ocean off North America in which more than 50 ships and 20 airplanes are said to have mysteriously disappeared. The area, whose boundaries are not universally agreed upon, has a triangular shape that reaches approximately from the Atlantic coast of Florida to Bermuda to the islands known as the Greater Antilles. Estimates of the Bermuda Triangle's total area range between 500,000 and 1,510,000 square miles.
